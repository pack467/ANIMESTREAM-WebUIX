Synopsis

Shirou Emiya is a high school student and the adopted son of Kiritsugu Emiya, who saved him from a fire that destroyed his home and killed his family. Kiritsugu, who was a magus, taught Shirou the basics of magic, but Shirou has little talent and can only perform simple spells.

One day, after school, Shirou witnesses a battle between two superhuman beings known as Servants. He is discovered and nearly killed, but is saved by a Servant of his own, Saber. With this, Shirou becomes a Master in the Fifth Holy Grail War, a secret magical tournament where seven mages and their Servants fight to the death for the Holy Grail, a legendary artifact that can grant any wish.
